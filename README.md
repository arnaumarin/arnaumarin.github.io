
# Hey, I'm Arnau
Welcome to my webpage! 

I’m a Ph.D. student at Harvard, focusing on brain-machine interfaces under the guidance of Prof. Jia Liu. Right now, I'm exploring ways for machines to learn and interact with the brain, using brain-inspired algorithms and brain-like electronics.

In the past, I earned my B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from UPC-Barcelona Tech, and I worked in cool places like Intel Labs / ZHAW, IDIBAPS, the University of Montreal, and the University of Utah, among others.
